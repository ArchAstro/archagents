# Onboarding Q&A — Sample Conversation

**New hire:** Jamie (Engineering, Day 2)

---

**Jamie:** What's the PTO policy?

**Agent:** You get 20 days of flexible PTO per year, no separate sick/personal buckets. There's no accrual — it's available from your start date, prorated for the calendar year.

Blackout periods: the last week of each quarter is discouraged for non-emergency PTO due to release cycles, but not blocked.

To request time off, submit through BambooHR at least 5 business days in advance. Your manager gets an approval notification automatically.

> Source: *Employee Handbook v4.2, Section 6.1 — Time Off*

---

**Jamie:** How do I submit an expense report?

**Agent:** Use Ramp (app or web). The process:

1. Upload receipt photo or forward the email receipt to receipts@ramp.com
2. Add the GL code — most engineering expenses fall under `ENG-5200` (equipment) or `ENG-5300` (software/subscriptions)
3. Submit for approval — goes to your manager for anything under $500, Finance for $500+

Reimbursements hit your paycheck within the next pay cycle. The deadline is 30 days from the expense date.

> Source: *Finance Policies — Expense Reimbursement (updated 2026-01)*

---

**Jamie:** Who should I talk to about getting access to the staging environment?

**Agent:** I don't have a definitive answer for your team's staging access process — it varies by group. Two things that should get you unblocked quickly:

1. **Check the #eng-access Slack channel** — there's a pinned message with the self-service access request form for most environments.
2. **Ask your onboarding buddy or manager** — they can add you directly if your team manages its own IAM roles.

If you need production-adjacent access (staging-prod), that requires a separate approval through the security team.
